* Principle of products
If $A_{1}, A_{2}, \cdots , A_{n}$ are finite [[file:Set.org][Sets]]
then $|A_{1} \times A_{2} \times \cdots \times A_{n}| = |A_1||A_2||A_3|\cdots|A_n|$
