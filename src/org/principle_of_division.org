* Principle of division
Let $A$ be a [[file:set.org][set]].

Let $R \subseteq A^2$ be a [[file:relation_of_equivalence.org][relation of equivalence]]

Let $A$ have $k$ [[file:class_of_equivalence.org][classes of equivalence]] and each class has a cardinality of m.

Then

\[m = \frac{|A|}{k}\]
