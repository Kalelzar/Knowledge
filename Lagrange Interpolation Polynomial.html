1 Lagrange Interpolation Polynomial

Let \(f\) be a function

Let \(y_i = f(x), i\in\overline{0,n}\),

Then the solution to Lagrange Interpolation Problem \(L_n(f;x)\) is called the Lagrange Interpolation Polynomial of degree \(\leq n\) for the function \(f\) and the knots of interpolation \(x_0, \dots, x_n\)
