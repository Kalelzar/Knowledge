1 Logical Equivalences

1.0.1 Constant's Rule

\(p \wedge T \equiv p\)

\(p \vee F \equiv p\)

\(p \vee T \equiv T\)

\(p \wedge F \equiv F\)

1.0.2 Negation Rule

\(p \land \neg p \equiv F\)

\(p \lor \neg{}p \equiv T\)

1.0.3 Identitity

\(p \lor p \equiv p\)

\(p \land p \equiv p\)

1.0.4 Double Negation Rule

\(\neg(\neg p)) \equiv p\)

1.0.5 Commutativity

\(p \lor q \equiv q \lor p\)

\(p \land q \equiv q \land p\)

1.0.6 Associativity

\((p \land q) \land r \equiv p \land ( q \land r)\)

\((p \lor q) \lor r \equiv p \lor ( q \lor r)\)

1.0.7 Distributivity

\(p \lor ( q \land r) \equiv (p \lor q) \land (p \lor r)\)

\(p \land ( q \lor r) \equiv (p \land q) \lor (p \land r)\)

1.0.8 Law of De Morgan

\(\neg (p \lor q) \equiv \neg p \land \neg q\)

\(\neg (p \land q) \equiv \neg p \lor \neg q\)

1.0.9 Swallowing

\(p \lor ( p \land q) \equiv p\)

\(p \land ( p \lor q) \equiv p\)

1.0.10 Property of the implication

\(p \rightarrow q \equiv \neg p \lor q\)

1.0.11 Property of the Bi-implication

\(p \Leftrightarrow q \equiv (p \rightarrow q) \land (q \rightarrow p)\)

Or also

\(p \Leftrightarrow q \equiv (\neg p \lor q) \land (\neg q \lor p)\)
