1 Relation

Let \(n>=1\) and \(A_{1}, A_{2}, \cdots, A_{n}\) are sets respectively called 'first domain', 'second domain', ⋯, 'nth-domain'

A relation over the Descartes Product \(A_{1} \times A_{2} \times \cdots \times A_{n}\) we call every set:

\[R \subseteq A_{1} \times A_{2} \times \cdots \times A_{n}\]

We say that R has an arity of n.
