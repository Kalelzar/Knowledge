1 Principle of inclusion and exclusion

\(\forall{}n\geq1, \forall\) sets \(A_{1}, A_{2}, \dots, A_{n}:\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} |A_1 \cup \cdots \cup A_{n}| &= \sum_{1\leq i \leq n}\limits|A_{i}| -\\ &- \sum_{1\leq i \leq j \leq n}\limits|A_{i}\cap{}A_{j}| +\\ &+ \cdots +\\ &+ (-1)^{n-1}|A_1 \cap \cdots \cap A_n| \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
