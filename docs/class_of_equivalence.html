1 Class of equivalence

Let \(R \in A^2\) be a Relation of Equivalence.

Then the class of equivalence of element \(a \in A\) are defined by the Set \([a] = \{ b \in A | aRb\}\).

The classes of equivalence of the relation are Partitions of the Set \(A\)
